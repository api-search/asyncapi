description: The wasmCloud lattice event system publishes CloudEvents-format messages to NATS subjects describing the lifecycle of components, capability providers, links, and hosts within a wasmCloud lattice. All lattice events are published on the wasmbus.evt.{lattice}.> subject hierarchy and conform to the CloudEvents 1.0 specification with a type prefix of com.wasmcloud.lattice. Events are used by the wash dashboard, wadm reconciler, observability tools, and custom integrations to react to changes in lattice state.
